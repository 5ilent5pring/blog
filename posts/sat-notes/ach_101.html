The Problem: Our Brains Cheat

When analysts see a new cyberattack, our brains instantly try to fit the data into a pattern we already know. This is called Satisficing.

The Solution: ACH

Analysis of Competing Hypotheses forces you to refute hypotheses rather than confirm them.

  1. List Hypotheses.
  2. List Evidence.
  3. Cross-reference them in a matrix.
